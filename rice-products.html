Red & Black Rice Varieties

Traditional Short-Grain Rice

Including Karunkuruvai, Kattuyanam, and Matti Arisi. Perfect for traditional dishes, idly, dosa, and everyday meals.

Varieties Include:
  • Karunkuruvai - Black rice variety
  • Kattuyanam - Ancient cultivar
  • Matti Arisi - Clay-grown rice
  • Idly Arisi - Perfect for fermentation
