Our Lentil Collection

Red Lentils (Masoor Dal)

Quick-cooking red lentils perfect for creamy dals and soups. Naturally sweet and mild, they cook quickly and are ideal for busy weeknight dinners.

Key Benefits:
  • Cooks in 15-20 minutes
  • High in protein
  • Rich in fiber
  • Easy to digest

Yellow Lentils (Chana Dal)

Creamy yellow lentils used extensively in South Indian cuisine. Great for dal, curries, and traditional recipes with mild, pleasant taste.

Key Benefits:
  • Mild and creamy
  • Rich in antioxidants
  • Excellent for curries
  • Great source of folate

Black Lentils (Urad Dal)

Whole black lentils with earthy flavor, perfect for dal makhani and traditional preparations. Essential for South Indian cuisine.

Key Benefits:
  • Rich, earthy flavor
  • Traditional dal makhani
  • High in calcium
  • Essential amino acids

Brown Lentils (Sabut Masoor)

Whole brown lentils with nutty flavor and firm texture. Perfect for salads and mixed vegetable dishes.

Key Benefits:
  • Firm texture maintained
  • Nutty flavor profile
  • Great for salads
  • High in polyphenols

Split Chickpeas (Chana Split)

Split chickpeas used in dal preparations and roasted snacks. Naturally sweet with creamy texture when cooked.

Key Benefits:
  • Very versatile
  • High in fiber
  • Creamy when cooked
  • Great for roasted snacks

Green Lentils (Moong Dal)

Quick-cooking green lentils with mild flavor. Perfect for sprouts, salads, and light dal preparations.

Key Benefits:
  • Light and easy to digest
  • Perfect for sprouts
  • Cooling in nature
  • Quick cooking time
