Our Sweetener Range

Country Sugar (Nattu Sarkarai)

Pure, crystallized sugar made from sugarcane juice without any chemicals or bleaching agents. An excellent alternative to white refined sugar.

Key Benefits:
  • Retains natural minerals
  • Rich in calcium and iron
  • Better digestion support
  • Perfect for beverages and cooking

Round Jaggery (Kundu Vellam)

Traditional round-shaped jaggery made from unrefined sugarcane juice, boiled and solidified in circular molds. A staple in Indian cuisine with incredible health benefits.

Key Benefits:
  • Aids digestion naturally
  • Excellent source of iron
  • Helps detoxify the liver
  • Supports respiratory health

Palm Jaggery (Panang Karupatti)

Premium jaggery extracted from palm sap, known for its rich dark color and unique caramel-like flavor. A premium sweetener with exceptional medicinal properties.

Key Benefits:
  • High in antioxidants
  • Supports bone health
  • Promotes skin health
  • Traditional cooling properties

Palm Sugar Candy (Panang Kandu)

Crystallized palm sugar in convenient candy form. Perfect for direct consumption, tea, or as a natural sweetener while maintaining all nutritional benefits.

Key Benefits:
  • Convenient ready-to-eat form
  • High mineral content
  • Energy boost snack
  • No artificial additives
